# Customer Privacy Policy (PRV-POL-001)

## 1. Objective

This policy establishes **[Company Name]**'s requirements for protecting customer privacy and providing required privacy notices in compliance with the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA) Privacy Rule (Regulation P), state privacy laws, and industry best practices. It ensures customers are informed about how their information is collected, used, shared, and protected.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- All nonpublic personal information (NPI) collected from consumers and customers
- All products and services offered by **[Company Name]**
- All channels through which customer information is collected
- All sharing of customer information with third parties
- Privacy notices and opt-out mechanisms
- All employees, contractors, and service providers handling customer information

## 3. Policy

**[Company Name]** shall protect customer privacy, provide required privacy notices, and honor customer choices regarding information sharing in compliance with GLBA and applicable state privacy laws.

### 3.1 Privacy Notice Requirements

Privacy notices shall be provided to customers as required by GLBA Regulation P.

**Initial Privacy Notice:**
- Provided at the time of establishing a customer relationship
- Delivered to consumers before disclosing NPI to nonaffiliated third parties (if not covered by an exception)
- Contains all required disclosures per Regulation P

**Annual Privacy Notice:**
- Provided to customers at least once during any 12-month period
- May use delivery exception if requirements are met (no sharing beyond exceptions, no changes to privacy practices)
- Delivered by a method reasonably expected to reach the customer

**Revised Privacy Notice:**
- Provided before sharing NPI in ways not previously disclosed
- Allows customers reasonable opportunity to opt out before sharing

**Notice Content Requirements:**
- Categories of NPI collected
- Categories of NPI disclosed
- Categories of affiliates and nonaffiliated third parties to whom NPI is disclosed
- Explanation of opt-out rights
- Description of how NPI is protected
- Required regulatory language

### 3.2 Opt-Out Rights

Customers shall be provided the right to opt out of certain information sharing.

**Opt-Out Required For:**
- Sharing NPI with nonaffiliated third parties (with exceptions)
- Affiliate marketing based on shared eligibility information

**Opt-Out Mechanism:**
- Clear and conspicuous opt-out notice
- Reasonable means to exercise opt-out (toll-free number, mail, online)
- Reasonable time to opt out before sharing (30 days minimum)
- Opt-out effective for duration of customer relationship

**Opt-Out Exceptions:**
- Service providers performing services on behalf of **[Company Name]**
- Joint marketing agreements with appropriate contractual protections
- Processing transactions requested by customer
- Reporting to consumer reporting agencies
- Other exceptions per Regulation P

**Opt-Out Administration:**
- Track customer opt-out elections
- Apply opt-out to all relevant sharing
- Process opt-out requests within reasonable timeframe
- Maintain opt-out records

### 3.3 Information Collection

Customer information shall be collected in accordance with disclosed practices.

**Collection Practices:**
- Collect only information necessary for business purposes
- Inform customers of information collection practices
- Collect information through legitimate means
- Document sources of customer information

**Types of Information Collected:**
- Information provided directly by customers (applications, transactions)
- Information from customer transactions
- Information from consumer reporting agencies
- Information from other sources (public records, third parties)

### 3.4 Information Use

Customer information shall be used only for disclosed and legitimate business purposes.

**Permitted Uses:**
- Processing customer transactions
- Servicing customer accounts
- Marketing **[Company Name]** products (unless opted out)
- Fraud prevention and security
- Legal and regulatory compliance
- Other disclosed purposes

**Use Limitations:**
- Information used only for purposes disclosed or permitted by law
- No selling of customer lists for third-party marketing without consent
- Marketing uses respect customer preferences

### 3.5 Information Sharing

Customer information sharing shall comply with privacy requirements.

**Affiliate Sharing:**
- May share NPI with affiliates for their everyday business purposes
- Marketing sharing based on eligibility information requires opt-out opportunity
- Affiliate sharing documented in privacy notice

**Nonaffiliated Third-Party Sharing:**
- Limited to disclosed purposes and exceptions
- Opt-out provided before sharing (unless exception applies)
- Contractual protections required for service providers
- Joint marketing agreements documented

**Service Provider Requirements:**
- Service providers contractually bound to maintain confidentiality
- Service providers limited to using NPI for contracted purposes
- Oversight of service provider compliance

### 3.6 Customer Access and Correction

Customers shall have the ability to access and correct their information.

**Access:**
- Customers may request access to their account information
- Access provided within reasonable timeframe
- Identity verification required before providing access

**Correction:**
- Customers may dispute inaccurate information
- Disputes investigated and resolved
- Corrections made as appropriate
- Customer notified of resolution

**FCRA Requirements:**
- Disputes regarding consumer report information handled per FCRA
- Adverse action notices provided as required

### 3.7 Information Retention and Disposal

Customer information shall be retained and disposed of appropriately.

**Retention:**
- Customer information retained per regulatory and business requirements
- Retention periods documented
- Information secured during retention period

**Disposal:**
- Customer information securely disposed when no longer needed
- Disposal methods prevent unauthorized access
- Disposal documented

See SEC-POL-004 (Data Classification and Handling Policy) for detailed disposal requirements.

### 3.8 State Privacy Law Compliance

**[Company Name]** shall comply with applicable state privacy laws.

**State Requirements:**
- Monitor state privacy law developments
- Implement additional requirements as applicable
- Maintain compliance with stricter state standards

**California Considerations:**
- CCPA/CPRA requirements for California residents (if applicable)
- Additional disclosures and rights as required

**Other State Laws:**
- Vermont requires affirmative consent for certain sharing
- Maine requires opt-in for internet service provider data
- Other state-specific requirements as applicable

### 3.9 Children's Privacy

Special protections apply to children's information.

**COPPA Compliance:**
- If applicable, comply with Children's Online Privacy Protection Act
- Obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting information from children under 13
- Implement required notices and controls

**General Practice:**
- **[Company Name]**'s services are not directed to children under 13
- No knowing collection of children's personal information without parental consent

### 3.10 Privacy Program Governance

A privacy program shall ensure ongoing compliance.

**Privacy Officer:**
- Designated privacy officer or function
- Responsibility for privacy policy and compliance
- Authority to address privacy issues

**Privacy Training:**
- Privacy training for all employees handling customer information
- Annual refresher training
- Role-based training for privacy-sensitive functions

**Privacy Assessments:**
- Periodic assessment of privacy practices
- New product/service privacy review
- Third-party privacy assessments as appropriate

## 5. Definitions

| **Term** | **Definition** |
|---|---|
| **Affiliate** | A company related by common ownership or control. |
| **Consumer** | An individual who obtains or has obtained a financial product or service for personal, family, or household purposes. |
| **Customer** | A consumer who has an ongoing relationship with the financial institution. |
| **Nonaffiliated Third Party** | Any entity that is not an affiliate of the financial institution. |
| **Nonpublic Personal Information (NPI)** | Personally identifiable financial information and any list derived from such information. |
| **Opt-Out** | A consumer's election to prevent certain sharing of their information. |
| **Privacy Notice** | A clear and conspicuous disclosure of privacy policies and practices. |
| **Service Provider** | A nonaffiliated third party that provides services to the financial institution. |
